About Dr. Shweta Mishra

Dr. Shweta Mishra heads the Diagnostic Laboratory at Satyanand Hospital, Shahjahanpur. She brings extensive experience in clinical pathology and diagnostic medicine, having previously served at Government Medical College (G.M.C.), Patiala.

Under her leadership, the hospital's laboratory offers comprehensive diagnostic services including hematology, biochemistry, microbiology, and histopathology. She ensures that every test result meets the highest standards of accuracy and reliability.
