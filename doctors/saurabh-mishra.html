About Dr. Saurabh Mishra

Dr. Saurabh Mishra is a Consultant in Anaesthesiology and Intensive Care at Satyanand Hospital, Shahjahanpur. With over 9 years of clinical experience, he specializes in critical care medicine, pain management, and perioperative anaesthesia for both routine and complex surgical procedures.

He holds an M.B.B.S. and M.D. in Anaesthesiology and is known for his calm demeanor during high-pressure situations in the ICU. His patient-centered approach ensures safe and comfortable surgical experiences for all patients.
