About Dr. Indu Yadav

Dr. Indu Yadav is the lead Specialist in Gynaecology and Obstetrics at Satyanand Hospital, Shahjahanpur. With 15 years of dedicated practice, she has guided thousands of women through pregnancy, childbirth, and reproductive health journeys.

She is committed to providing comprehensive women's healthcare with compassion and expertise, ensuring every patient feels safe, informed, and empowered throughout their care.

Education & Training

  • M.B.B.S. in Obstetrics and Gynecology
  • M.D. in Obstetrics and Gynecology — Specialization in comprehensive women's care
  • Fellowship in Reproductive Medicine (FRM) — Advanced fertility treatment
  • Diploma in Minimal Access Surgery (DMAS) — Minimally invasive procedures

Areas of Expertise

  • Normal & Cesarean Deliveries
