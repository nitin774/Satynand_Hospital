Frequently Ask Questions?

FAQs for Patients

What are the visiting hours for patients and their families?
Please check visiting hours at the hospital reception.
How do I schedule an appointment with a doctor?
It's easy! You can schedule an appointment by calling our hospital reception or booking online through our website.
Are there any visiting hours for patients?
Yes, we have visiting hours to ensure patients get rest. You can check visiting hours at the hospital reception.
How can I know about the facilities available at the hospital?
You can find information about our facilities on our website or inquire at the hospital reception.
What if I have a medical emergency outside regular hours?
Don't worry! We have a dedicated emergency department that operates 24/7. Just come in or call for immediate assistance.
Can I get updates on my relative's condition during their stay?
Absolutely! You can inquire about your relative's condition from our nursing staff or ask for updates from the attending doctor. We understand your concern and will keep you informed.

Health Tips and Resources

Stay Healthy

Eat a Balanced Diet:

Include a variety of foods like fruits, vegetables, grains, and proteins in your meals. Opt for homemade meals and limit intake of sugary and processed foods.

Stay Active:

Engage in regular physical activity like walking, jogging, or cycling for at least 30 minutes a day. Exercise helps maintain weight, improves mood, and boosts overall health.

Stay Hydrated:

Drink plenty of water throughout the day to keep your body hydrated. Limit intake of sugary drinks and opt for water, coconut water, or homemade juices.

Prioritize Mental Health:

Take time for relaxation and stress management. Practice deep breathing, meditation, or hobbies you enjoy to reduce stress and improve mental well-being.
